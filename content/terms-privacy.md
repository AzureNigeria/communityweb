By participating in Azure Nigeria Community events and discussions, you agree to maintain a respectful, inclusive environment. We reserve the right to remove disruptive content or behavior.

We respect your privacy. We only collect information needed to run community events, newsletters, and communications. We never sell your data and you can opt out at any time by emailing [hello@azurenigeria.org](mailto:hello@azurenigeria.org).
