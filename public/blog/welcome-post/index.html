Welcome to Azure Nigeria Community

Community Jan 05, 2025 Azure Nigeria Team

Azure Nigeria Community is a thriving community of developers, cloud enthusiasts, and tech professionals passionate about Microsoft Azure and cloud technologies. Our mission is to build, nurture, and showcase Nigerian talent in the global cloud computing space.

What We Do

  • Instructor-led training and hands-on workshops from Azure fundamentals to advanced services
  • Real-world projects to build solutions with Azure technologies
  • Networking and collaboration with fellow developers and industry professionals
  • Knowledge sharing with experienced Azure practitioners and Microsoft MVPs
  • Career development and certification prep for cloud roles

How to Get Involved

Join our Meetup, attend sessions, and start building with the community. Follow us on LinkedIn and Twitter/X for updates and event announcements.
