FAQ

Answers before you apply

What does the voucher cover?

The voucher covers the exam fee for the listed certifications only, issued after verification.

Can I apply if I am not ready by March 27?

No. This cohort is only for candidates who can meet the exam deadline and program requirements.

How is eligibility decided?

Eligibility is earned by completing all four requirements and passing the exam.

What proof do I need to submit?

Submit proof of exam result after you pass to qualify for the voucher draw.

Can I change certifications after applying?

Slot allocations are fixed. Changes may not be possible due to limited availability.
